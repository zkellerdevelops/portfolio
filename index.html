Hello. My name is Zach Keller. I've been working tirelessly as a graphic designer in Nashville, TN and Atlanta, GA for the last 9 years. I've had the pleasure of working in an Atlanta startup agency called Foundation One. I've opened my own design business called Identity Crisis with my best friend, and most recently, I've worked a three year stint in an in house design team in the publishing industry.

I've just finished Nashville Software School, a 6 month web developer bootcamp to take my visual design skills to the next level, and am looking forward to shifting gears to this next chapter of my design and development career. Currently, I'm available for quick freelance, long-term contract, or full-time web development, print, and identity design work.

Have a look at my work and say hello. I love tacos, the Grateful Dead, and Tennessee football. Looking forward to hearing from you.
